Bolgatty Palace

Bolgatty Palace is a historic Dutch palace located on Bolgatty Island in Kochi, surrounded by lush greenery and calm backwaters. Built in the 18th century, it is one of the oldest existing Dutch palaces outside the Netherlands and stands as a symbol of Kochi’s colonial heritage.

Today, the palace functions as a heritage hotel while retaining its classic architecture and charm. Its scenic location, peaceful surroundings, and views of the backwaters make Bolgatty Palace a popular destination for travelers interested in history, architecture, and luxury experiences.
