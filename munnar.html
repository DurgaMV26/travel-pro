Munnar Sightseeing

Explore the most loved attractions of Munnar with travelX

Eravikulam National Park

Nestled in the rolling hills of Munnar, Eravikulam National Park is a haven of natural beauty and one of Kerala’s most treasured wildlife destinations. Known for protecting the rare and endangered Nilgiri Tahr, the park welcomes visitors with vast green meadows, cloud-covered valleys, and refreshing mountain breezes that instantly calm the mind.As you explore the park, you’ll be greeted by panoramic views of the Western Ghats, endless grasslands, and seasonal blooms that paint the landscape in vibrant colors. The cool climate and well-maintained pathways make it ideal for leisurely walks, nature photography, and peaceful sightseeing away from city noise. On clear days, the elevated viewpoints offer breathtaking vistas that stretch across Munnar’s misty hills.

Munnar Tea Gardens

Stretching across the misty hills of Munnar, the tea gardens create a breathtaking landscape of endless green waves. These lush plantations offer a refreshing escape where cool mountain air, gentle slopes, and neatly lined tea bushes set a peaceful rhythm. Walking through the gardens, visitors can enjoy scenic trails, panoramic viewpoints, and glimpses of local tea pluckers at work, adding charm to the experience. Perfect for nature walks, photography, and quiet moments, Munnar Tea Gardens capture the true essence of hill-station beauty.

Mattupetty Dam

Set amidst the rolling hills of Munnar, Mattupetty Dam is a serene retreat known for its calm waters and scenic surroundings. The reservoir reflects the lush green hills and drifting clouds, creating a peaceful atmosphere perfect for relaxation. Visitors can enjoy boating on the lake, spot occasional wildlife along the banks, or simply soak in the cool mountain breeze. With its tranquil setting and panoramic views, Mattupetty Dam is an ideal stop for nature lovers, couples, and families seeking quiet moments in Munnar’s refreshing landscape.

Echo Point

Located amid Munnar’s misty hills, Echo Point is a charming natural attraction where your voice playfully echoes back from the surrounding cliffs. Set beside a calm lake and lush green slopes, the spot offers refreshing mountain air and scenic views that make it a favorite among visitors. Whether you’re enjoying a leisurely walk, capturing photographs, or simply experiencing the fun echo phenomenon, Echo Point adds a touch of joy and wonder to your Munnar journey.

Kundala Lake

Tucked away among Munnar’s rolling hills, Kundala Lake is a peaceful retreat known for its calm waters and scenic surroundings. Framed by lush green slopes and seasonal cherry blossoms, the lake offers a refreshing escape from the busy town. Visitors can enjoy pedal boating on the tranquil waters, take relaxed walks along the shore, or simply soak in the cool mountain breeze. With its quiet charm and picture-perfect views, Kundala Lake is an ideal spot for couples, families, and nature lovers seeking serene moments in Munnar.

Kolukkumalai Peak

Perched high above the clouds, Kolukkumalai is home to the world’s highest tea plantation and offers one of the most breathtaking sunrise views in South India. Surrounded by rugged mountains and endless layers of mist, this remote destination feels untouched and truly magical. Visitors can experience thrilling jeep rides, explore traditional tea estates, and witness golden sunlight slowly illuminating the hills. The cool climate, fresh mountain air, and dramatic landscapes make Kolukkumalai a must-visit for adventure seekers, photographers, and nature lovers exploring Munnar.
