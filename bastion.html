Bastion Bungalow

Bastion Bungalow is a historic colonial-era building located along the Fort Kochi seaside. Once used by European officials, the bungalow reflects classic colonial architecture and offers beautiful views of the Arabian Sea.

Today, Bastion Bungalow stands as a heritage landmark that highlights Kochi’s colonial past and maritime importance. Its calm surroundings and scenic location make it a popular stop for travelers exploring the cultural and historical sites of Fort Kochi.
